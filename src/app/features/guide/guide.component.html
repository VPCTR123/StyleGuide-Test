When buttons are instantly recognizable and easy to understand, an app tends to feel intuitive and well designed. Make buttons easy for people to use. It’s essential to include enough space around a button so that people can visually distinguish it from surrounding components and content. Giving a button enough space is also critical for helping people select or activate it, regardless of the method of input they use. As a general rule, a button needs a hit region of at least 60x60 pt — to ensure that people can select it easily, whether they use a fingertip or a pointer.
